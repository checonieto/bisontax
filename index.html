Maximize Your Tax Refund

Our experts identify every deduction and credit you qualify for to ensure you get the maximum refund possible

Child Tax Credits

We help you claim up to $2,000 per qualifying child, plus additional credits for child care expenses that can save you hundreds more.

Education Credits

American Opportunity Credit and Lifetime Learning Credit can provide up to $2,500 per student for qualified education expenses.

Earned Income Tax Credit

Business Expenses

Charitable Contributions

What You'll Need

To ensure a smooth tax preparation process, please have these documents ready for your appointment

Required Documents

  • Prior Year Tax Return: Your completed tax return from last year
  • W-2 Forms: From all employers for the current tax year
  • 1099 Forms: For freelance work, contract jobs, or investment income
  • Social Security Numbers: For yourself, spouse, and all dependents
  • Income Documents: Any other records of income received
  • Deduction Records: Receipts for donations, medical expenses, etc.
  • Business Expenses: If self-employed, all business expense records
  • Education Expenses: Tuition statements (Form 1098-T)
  • Health Insurance Info: Form 1095-A, B, or C if applicable
  • Retirement Contributions: IRA or HSA contribution records

Don't worry if you're missing some documents! We can still begin the process and help you track down missing information.
